 Local Facilities & Island Views

Villa Nima is located in a convenient position to enjoy local facilities. Within easy walking distance of the villa there are various restaurants, shops, supermarkets and traditional taverna.

 

 

 

You can find good traditional food at the local tavernas, but also a short drive away are many options for dining out. The local beach is Lourdas which can be reached by foot (for the energetic), short car journey or local bus service.

 

 

Lourdas has an excellent sand/pebble beach front for sunbathing and swimming. In addition there are tavernas where you can eat sit and relax whilst enjoying the sea view over Lourda bay. 

 

 

In the village our favourites and most guests are Andromeda, Olive Lounge and La Mer.  For a cold drink, snack and lovely host pop into Afrodites.  Down on the local beach you will enjoy the delights of Taverna Klismata and lose yourself by the sea views .......
