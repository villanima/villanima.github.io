Welcome to Villa Nima and its gorgeous views             

 

A special place to relax and enjoy the delights of Kefalonia

 

 

A uniquely designed private luxury villa. The villa offersthe highest standard of furnished accommodation and finishes.

 

 

It can be found in Vlahata, a lovely Greek village which is local to the popular Lourdas beach area on the beautiful island of Kefalonia.

 

 

The Villa boasts panoramic and stunning views of the bay and sea coves. Nima has its own exclusive pool, stands in pretty grounds and comprises 2 large double bedrooms, 1 large twin bedroom, all with luxury on-suite shower/wet rooms.

 

 

Fully air conditioned, the villa has a large open plan living area with modern facilities inclusive of LCD TV, DVD and I Pod/ CD music system.  FREE Unlimited WIFI Access.  The dining area comprises natural oak furniture.

 

 

Open plan kitchen is fitted with modern oven, hob, fridge/freezer, microwave, washing machine & dishwasher.

 

 

Personal Chef Service available !

 

 

Externally: private balconies, patios, sun terraces, 8 sun loungers, landscaped garden, family barbecue and pergola in tranquil setting.

 

 

Villa Nima is fully EOT licensed with the Greek National Tourist Organisation for user safety and compliance.
